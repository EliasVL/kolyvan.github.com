--- layout: kybook_post title: How to move or upload a book? ---

There are two methods to move or upload an ebook or PDF document.

  • Long tap the book and select from appeared menu the ‘Move’ item. Then select a destination bookshelf, cloud service or netwrok folder.
  • Tap the ‘Edit’ button on a navigation bar, then tap books for selecting them. Further tap the ‘Move’ button on navigation bar and select a destination bookshelf, cloud service or netwrok folder.
