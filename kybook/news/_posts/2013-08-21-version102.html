--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 1.0.2 ---

KyBook v1.0.2 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Hyphenation for german, french, spanish, italian, portuguese and polish languages.
  • Improved loading books from OPDS catalogs and Cloud Storages.
  • TXT format.
  • Improved support of FB2 format.
  • Added checking of a network connection availability.
  • Speed up ebook opening.
