--- layout: kybook_post title: Version 1.5.2 ---

KyBook v1.5.2 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Fixed a crash that could occur while reading PDF documents.
  • Fixed an issue with display of previously deleted items in Google.Drive cloud.
  • Simplified an activation of a page spread mode.
  • Setting for an action after downloading ebook (show a message, play a sound, open a book).
  • Menu (delete, open, select all, download) for files in the clouds and local network.
  • Editable title of bookmarks.
  • Tags for books (search by tag, editing).
  • Ability to search local OPDS catalogs (Calibre).
