KyBook v1.6.2 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • CHM scaling
  • Added Microsoft Translator as an option instead of Yandex.Translate
  • Improved support of SMB protocol
  • Optimization of DJVU rendering
  • Setting to remove DJVU background layer
  • Fixed a bug in the hyphenation algorithm for texts in Russian
  • Fixed a crash when searching the web covers
