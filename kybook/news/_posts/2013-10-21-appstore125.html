KyBook v1.2.5 is now available on the App Store.

What's New in this Version

  • Sort the books by Series
  • Ability to share notes for a book
  • Highlighting the current chapter in the table of contents
  • Customizable background color for the cards on the bookshelf
  • Profiles (day/night mode)
  • Various bug fixes
